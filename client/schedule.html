How to schedule Messages

Choose when and for how long messages are displayed.

The configuration is simple

💌 Write a lovely message you'd like to share
👀 Find the "Advanced" link below the "Send" button.
🔧 The shown configuration will be the standard setting.

Changing the duration a message is shown

👷🏼‍♂️ If you want to change the duration a message is shown,
🔧 click on the selection menue below the label "Duration"
🎣 Pick the option that fits you the most
🚀 And you are done! Click on send to take the effect!

🤹 You can of course combine this effect with others.

Changing the date of the message

👷🏼‍♂️ If you want to change the date where the message is send,
🔧 click on the selection menue below the label "Schedule"
🎣 Pick the option that fits you the most
🚀 And you are done! Click on send to take the effect!

🤹 You can of course combine this effect with others.
